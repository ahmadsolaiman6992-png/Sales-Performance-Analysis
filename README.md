Title:
Sales Performance Analysis
Main Question:
What are the best and worst performing products, and is there a growth trend in sales?
Tools:
Python, pandas, seaborn, matplotlib
Key Findings:
75% of profits are below 9,935, with some high-value outliers present.
Clothing is the top category for attracting new customers.
Furniture generates the highest overall profit across most regions.
The South branch shows significantly lower sales compared to other regions, requiring further investigation.
